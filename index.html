Mastermind

How To Play

  • Guess the pattern generated by the computer in 8 turns or less to win the game! The pattern is a random combination of 4 colored pins

  • The pattern can have repeating colors.

  • Make a guess by placing a row of code pins on the decoding board.

  • Once you click "check" computer will provide feedback by placing zero to four key pins in the small holes to the right.

  • A black key pin is placed for each pin that is correct in both color and position.

  • A white key pin is placed for each pin that is correct in color but wrong in position.

  • The order of the key pins don't matter.
